About Me

Hi! I am Rajyashree, a.k.a Rai (pronounced rye - as in the whiskey). I'm a neuroscientist interested in understanding the neuronal mechanisms underlying social behaviors. If I'm not in the lab or analyzing data, I like to write and do improv comedy. I was born and raised in India, but currently I live in New York City.
